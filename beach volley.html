Beach Volley

Beach Volley is leisurely game where you play as a blobby softbody water animal or bird and compete against your counterparts in a friendly game of beach volleyball.

About Beach Volley-

Inspired by low-poly art-style, Beach Volley is a simple, two player sports battle. Currently, players can compete against computer, with the multiplayer online and PVP modes coming soon. This game was developed as a project in my internship.


Beach Volley is now available on Google Play Store for android devices.
